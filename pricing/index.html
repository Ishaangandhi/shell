Pricing

Free

Shell Notebook may be downloaded and used for free for trial use. There is no time limit for trial use, and the trial version is fully featured. The trial version includes occasional reminders to purchase a license key.

$39

An individual license must be purchased for extended use. Upon successful purchase of a license, a key will be emailed to you. You can add this key to the Shell Notebook app in the license menu to unlock the full version.
